◆ METHODOLOGY

How the calculator works

Default cost = Volume × Default emission intensity × (1 + mark-up%) × ETS price × CBAM phase-in rate.

Actual cost = Volume × your actual emission intensity × ETS price × CBAM phase-in rate — with no mark-up.

Mark-up ramp: +10% (2026) → +20% (2027) → +30% (2028+). CBAM phase-in: 2.5% → 48.5% by 2030 → 100% by 2034.

Benchmark deductions (free allocation equivalents) are not modeled here. Actual savings are typically larger once the EU benchmark is applied. These figures are illustrative — not legal or tax advice.

◆ ABOUT

Built by people who've stood on a factory floor.

AuraCap started from a simple observation: most Jordanian and MENA factories know they have a carbon problem, but the solutions offered to them don't fit.

Global ESG consultancies charge €100k+ for engagements that end with a paper report. Local energy auditors deliver ISO 50001 reports that stop at kWh savings. Both miss the moment we're in — where carbon is becoming a financial liability, a trade barrier, and a loan condition all at once.

AuraCap was designed for that exact gap. We're a hybrid company: our hardware goes onto the shop floor, our software captures the data, and our methodology translates it into the language that CFOs, banks, and EU importers can all use.

Our belief

You cannot manage what you don't measure. And what is measured incorrectly is managed worse. Every carbon decision a factory makes today will be audited tomorrow — by regulators, by buyers, by lenders. The factories that survive this decade will be the ones that built operational truth early.
